ABOUT US

RESPECT

Our teachers treat the students with respect, giving constructive corrections and helping them develop accordingly.

FLOORING

To protect your children, our dance floors are sprung wood with a professional non-slip dance surface installed.

STUDIO

Our studio offers a newly constructed professional setting that is clean, safe, comfortable and inviting.

CONVENIENCE

With our conveniently scheduled evening and weekend hours you are sure to find a class that fits perfectly into your schedule. We are located in the shopping center on the northeast corner of Long Lake @ Livernois surrounded by Kroger, UPS, Hallmark, CVS, etc…

NURTURING

Proper fostering and mentoring throughout your child’s dance education will help them develop at a comfortable stride on pace with your families goals.

PRIDE

We are proud to offer professional dance training in a nurturing, family environment. We work hard to ensure that you are proud to be a part of this dance family and willing to refer to your friends.

Jazz

Encompassing many styles including classic, video and contemporary the fast pace and upbeat tempo of our jazz classes make this an exciting experience for students of all ages.

Tap

Drawn from several styles of tap including rhythm, soft shoe and tacit our tap classes allow students to explore rhythm and tempo while gaining a strong understanding of tap technique.

Ballet

Our ballet classes focus on classical Russian technique, which provides students not only with a strong foundation for other forms of dance but also poise and grace.

Pre-Ballet

An Introduction to basic ballet techniques. This class also helps young students improve motor skills and coordination. This class is a great way to introduce youngsters to dance.

Lyrical

Lyrical classes provide experienced students with an opportunity to further explore their movement vocabulary with a class that truly combines ballet and jazz
(prior ballet experience is strongly recommended)

Hip-Hop

Our hip-hop classes focus on contemporary movement set to contemporary hip-hop an pop music. This class is great not only for dancers looking to explore a new technique but also for beginners looking for some great exercise.

PROGRAMS

At Juliart we are proud to offer several different training programs to suit your dancer’s interest level and goals. Within all of our programs, we keep class sizes small in an effort to provide focused, individualized attention to each dancer.

RECREATIONAL DANCE

Our recreational classes are for every dancer at every level. Whether you are interested in having your 3 year old try ballet for the first time or your 12 year old continue to study hip hop and lyrical we have classes to fit their needs. We also offer adult classes if you are looking for a fun way to get fit. All of our recreational classes perform in our annual recital in June and enjoy the excitement that dancing onstage brings.

PERFORMANCE DANCE

The Juliart Performance group is a group of dancers who have expressed an interest in becoming more involved in performing and competing. These dancers take jazz, ballet, and lyrical, as well as an additional technique class. This year the dancers competed at two competitions, Starquest and Headliner, where they received high golds. This group provides an excellent opportunity to not only further your dance training but also explore other performance opportunities. The performance group dancers are selected after an audition in the spring.

COMPANY DANCE

Juliart Company Dancers, our pre-professional dancers ranging in ages from 9 to 18, are chosen by audition. These dancers spend many hours per week at the studio, not only taking extra classes, but also following an intense rehearsal schedule. In addition to their time at the studio, these girls also get the opportunity to train with some of the nation’s top choreographers. These dedicated dancers compete locally February through May and train year round.
